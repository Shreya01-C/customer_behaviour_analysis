# Customer Behaviour Analysis
--- 
## Overview
This project focuses on analyzing customer purchasing behavior to uncover insights that drive revenue growth, customer retention, and marketing optimization. By studying customer responses across demographics, product categories, discounts, and subscription models, we aim to understand how different factors influence overall company performance.

---
## Business Problem
Retail companies often struggle with:
- Low customer retention
- Limited understanding of product demand
- Ineffective customer segmentation
- Seasonal demand fluctuations
- Unclear payment behavior patterns
This project addresses these challenges by leveraging data-driven analysis to provide actionable insights.
---
## Methodology
- Data Collection
  - Customer transaction records
  - Demographic information (age, gender, subscription status)
  - Product details (category, ratings, discounts, shipping type)
- Data Cleaning & Preprocessing
  - Handling missing values
  - Normalizing categorical variables
  - Creating derived features (e.g., loyalty segments, discount usage rate)
---
## Tech Stack
- Python (Pandas)
- MySQL for structured queries
- Jupyter Notebook for analysis and visualization
- Power BI for dashboard reporting
---
## Outcomes
- Clear understanding of customer purchase patterns
- Identification of revenue-driving categories and products
- Insights into discount effectiveness
- Segmentation of customers for targeted marketing
- Recommendations to improve retention and subscription rates
---
